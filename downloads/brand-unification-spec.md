# TarotPulse Brand Unification — Option A: The Fusion
*Draft spec, 2026-06-04. For Matt + Jaye Anne. "The heirloom grimoire that's secretly a data instrument."*

## Thesis
The brand thesis is in the name: **Tarot (ancient — gold, serif, filigree) + Pulse (the signal/data-line — the techno-mystic signature).** The product is divination-as-data (*Pattern-Hunter, Stalker Card, Symbol Map, "track your trends, see your patterns"*). The antique-grimoire look sells and earns mystical credibility; the techno signature is what makes TarotPulse a category-of-one instead of one more pretty tarot journal. **Keep her grimoire as the dominant skin; thread the signal through it.**

Rule: navy+gold do 90% of the work; **lavender is the rare signal accent, never a third primary.**

## Type pairing
- **Display / titles:** classical serif caps — **Cinzel** (or Cormorant Garamond). Her grimoire register. *(Retire Silkscreen pixel from titles.)*
- **Data layer / eyebrows / field labels / Symbol Map:** **Space Mono** — the "terminal" signal layer (e.g. `TRACK / DECODE / TRANSFORM`).
- **Body:** a humanist serif (Cormorant/Lora) for warmth, OR keep Space Grotesk for UI/app surfaces.
- **Silkscreen pixel:** demote to a *tiny logo-adjacent signature only* — not body, not titles.

## Ornament system
- **Primary:** antique gold celestial line-art — filigree borders, hand-holding-card, third eye, zodiac/alchemical glyphs (her existing language).
- **Signal weave:** filigree **transitions into fine circuit-traces** at corners/edges; **constellations rendered as connected luminous nodes** (a star map that doubles as a data graph); the **pulse/EKG waveform** as a recurring divider/spine motif (it's the logo mark — make it structural, not decorative).
- Lavender glow only along the pulse-line / third eye.

## Logo
TarotPulse pulse-emblem + gold wordmark, centered foot of covers. The **pulse-waveform is the through-line motif** across cover → interior → app → audio (the EKG waveform already in the audio-guide art).

## Voice register
